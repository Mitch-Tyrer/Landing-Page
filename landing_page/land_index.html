Mitchell Tyrer, Cinematographer

A freelance Cinematographer and Photographer in the Denver, Colorado area. I am classically trained in analog film and have been sporadically making videos and films for ten years now. Tech savvy and fearless, I am a great addition to any project big or small as I love to collaborate and help bring stories to life.

I have been pursing film and video work since I was a senior in high school (2007), shooting and editing both a feature documentary and a feature length narrative film as part of their video curriculum. From there I went to Columbia College Chicago where I obtained my Bachelor's in Film and Video Production concentrating in Cinematography (2011). What made Columbia a great choice was that I was taught by working instead of just the theory, as well as requiring me to learn all aspects of the production process by doing several jobs on different collaborative experiences. Spending most of my college career on a sound stage in either the camera and lighting departments, taught me many skills as well has how to think on your feet and solve unforeseen problems quickly.
